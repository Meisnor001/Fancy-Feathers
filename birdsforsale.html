Canary

Bred for their beautiful color and singing voice, Canaries are social birds and can live in flocks. Bright and musical, they originally came from the Canary Islands, and are the perfect bird for beginners and make fun and interesting pets. Canaries can grow to 5 inches and live to be 15 years old. Canaries like to watch their pet parent and be whistled to but prefer not to be handled.
